This task requires a screen resolution bigger than 800x600. Please make your browser window big enough to see the task.

This task tests your visual short-term memory.

The task consists of 30 trials. Each trial takes about 5 seconds.

Your results will be automatically submitted after you finish the task.

You are first going to learn about the task by going through a demo of a single trial.

Before you start, we need to check whether your browser window is large enough.

Please press the button below.

The task requires the ability to see color or perceive color differences.

Below, in each picture you'll see a number. Type the number in the blank.

Click 'Check My Color Vision' button at the bottom once you are done.






Thank you. Your color vision is fine.

Now we will go through a demo trial. Click Continue to start.

Each trial will start with a blank screen.

Stimuli will always appear on the center of the gray screen.

First, you will very briefly see a colored oval with a random orientation and a color.

Remember its ORIENTATION. You can ignore the color.

Then a blank screen will appear for one second.
Then, another oval will appear on the center of the screen. Your job is to reproduce the orientation of the oval that you saw previously.

You can change the orientation by moving the mouse. When you are done, press the spacebar. You can try it now.

At the end of the trial, you can compare the correct orientation (top) with your reponse (bottom, "Reported").

This is the end of the demo. To start the task, press Next.

You will now start the task.

Be prepared! The displays will be VERY brief.
